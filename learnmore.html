FACTS

Homelessness is a big issues in America. We always see them but we never really know their story. Here in NYC, the number of homeless people has skyrocketed, reaching one of it highest levels since the 1930s Great Depression ; studies show that in June 2018, there were approximately 61,421 people in Shelters every night. Of those 61,421 people, 22,266 were children in families, 22,256 were adults that have families, and 16,899 were single adults. The number of homeless people sleeping in shelters has increased by 85% over the course of ten years. The main cause of homelessness is unaffordable housing. Other major reasons includes - eviction, doubled-up or severely overcrowded housing, domestic violence, job loss, and hazardous housing conditions.
