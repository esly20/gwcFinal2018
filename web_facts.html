FUN FACTS ABOUT BUFFALOS

  • SCIENTIFIC NAME: Bubalus bubalis
  • DIET: Herbivores
  • AVERAGE LIFE SPAN IN THE WILD: Up to 25 years
  • SIZE: Head and body, 8 to 9 ft; tail 2 to 3.3 ft
  • True buffalo only live in Asia and Africa.(The rest are obviously fake.)
  • Wild water buffalo are at-risk and live only in a small number of protected areas stretching across India, Nepal, and Bhutan, and a wildlife reserve in Thailand.
